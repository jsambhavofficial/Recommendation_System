## 💡 Findings & Recommendations

### 1. Model Selection
**Linear SVM** is the superior model for this task. It consistently outperforms Logistic Regression, KNN, and Naive Bayes across all feature spaces. It handles the high-dimensional sparse data generated by TF-IDF exceptionally well.

### 2. Feature Engineering (N-Grams)
*   **Unigrams (1-1 Gram)** achieved the absolute highest accuracy. This suggests that specific individual words (like "Python", "React", "Cybersecurity") are the strongest indicators of domain.
*   However, **BiGrams (1-2 Gram)** are recommended for the production `main.py` because they capture phrases (e.g., "Machine Learning", "Data Science") which are common in user queries.

### 3. Scaling (Max Features)
The jump from 500 to 1500 features improved accuracy significantly for most models. The current setting of **1500 features** in `main.py` is well-justified.
